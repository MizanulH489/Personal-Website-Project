Sports Gallery

These are a small display of the many different martial arts present in MMA that we will cover

BJJ-Brazilian Jiu Jitsu


One of the most influential martial arts in history, BJJ originates from Japanese Jiu Jitsu and Judo meeting Brazilian wrestling

Muay Thai


The national sport of Thailand, as well as one of the most popular contact sports in combat. A rather brutal style, utilizng all limbs of the body, as well as boxing and kickboxing

Boxing


An ancient sport that has been practiced since the days of the Greek Olympics, to modern day olympics, and modern boxing.

Wrestling


One of the most widely popular and favored sports in human history, consisting of basic grappling and takedowns

Sambo


Developed in the USSR as a form of hand to hand combat for the Red Soviet army, this combat martial arts soon spread in popularity. Using a mix of Judo, BJJ, Wrestling, and some form of striking, it is known for it's effectiveness in combat

Dutch Kickboxing


Born from Dutch Kickboxers training in Japan, this infamous style uses a combination of Muay Thai, American boxing, and typical kickboxing, along with Karate.
