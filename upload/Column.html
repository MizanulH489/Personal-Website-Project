MMA and Boxing organizations/Companies

These are some of the MMA organizations and Boxing Organization that we follow and produce news on, from UFC to WBO.

UFC

Originating in the 1980s, the UFC was one of the first organizations to rise to promincence in America. This MMA organization took America by the storm as it it grew to popularity, showing itself as the proving ground to show which martial art was the greatest.

WBO/WBC

These two are the modern succesors of the many Boxing organizations that has hosted many of the greats. Boxing had always been a mainstream sport in the american culture, seeing the likes of Muhammad Ali, Mike Tyson, and many others.

Smaller Organizations

Some of the smaller organizations/sports we cover includes the many grappling organizations/competitions, Muay thai, Glory Kickboxing, etc. These soon grew to prominence in their own niche.
